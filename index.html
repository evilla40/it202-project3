In this side-scrolling game, you play as a Pokemon, Sylveon. Your goal is to collect as many bows as possible, collecting points, while avoiding the skulls that take away your lives. Press the left and right arrows to move around. The game is over when you lose all your hearts. Press the up arrow to start the game.
